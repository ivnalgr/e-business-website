About GameG

GameG is a brand new digital distrubition platform for gaming, which aims to put customers first by providing as much value as possible in return. With a wide variety of games and applications from AAA to indie publishers, GameG is the place to go!

Made up of 5 new entreprenuers who have a passion for gaming and its community. Together, Aplhonese, Ivan, Ernest, Wenhui, and Robert came together to create and develop a new online marketplace specifically tailored to benefit fellow gamers and creators called GameG.

About Game-G

GameG is an online marketplace that Team Radiant has created for users to purchase and play games as well as to interact with others online. In the same vein as other online gaming platforms such as Steam and Epic Games, GameG promotes and releases various different games from established and indie companies alike along with the intent of allowing cross platform compatibility. GameG, as a business, was created because we, as gamers who play in different platforms, feel that we should all be able to play across different platforms together.
